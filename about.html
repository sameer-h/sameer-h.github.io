about me

Much of my elementary and middle school years were in Asia/Africa/Europe where I was exposed to a multitude of cultures/peoples that have all given me a very unique and global perspective. I'm glad for the travels I've had and also very grateful to now settle down in the US.

I'm now a Computer Science student at Texas A&M and am eager to learn about and work in Tech. I'm hardworking, driven, and a great communicator that's always looking to improve.
